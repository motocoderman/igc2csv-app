  Privacy Policy for igc2csv

  Last updated: February 3, 2026

  Overview

  igc2csv is a file conversion utility that converts IGC (International Gliding Commission) flight log files to
  CSV format. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

  Data Collection

  igc2csv does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically:

  - No personal information is collected - We do not collect names, email addresses, or any identifying
  information.
  - No usage analytics - We do not track how you use the app.
  - No crash reporting - We do not collect crash logs or diagnostics.
  - No third-party services - The app does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  - No network connections - The app operates entirely offline and makes no internet connections.

  File Processing

  All IGC file processing occurs locally on your device. Your flight log files are never uploaded to any server.
  The converted CSV files are saved to your device and remain under your control.

  Data Storage

  The app does not store any data beyond the CSV files you create through conversion. On iOS, converted files are
  saved to the app's documents folder until you export or delete them. On macOS, files are saved to the location
  you specify.

  Children's Privacy

  The app does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.

  Changes to This Policy

  If this privacy policy changes, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

  Contact

  If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: igc2csv@icloud.com
